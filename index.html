Our Story

A Century of Hearth & Harvest

Shaila Nafees Farmhouse isn't just a destination; it's a living history. Founded by the Miller family in 1892, these 150 acres have witnessed generations of dawn harvests and twilight celebrations.

We’ve meticulously restored the original stonework and timber beams of the manor, ensuring that while you enjoy modern plush bedding and high-speed connectivity, you never lose that soul-stirring connection to the past.

"Here, time doesn't fly. It wanders through the meadows and rests by the fire."
